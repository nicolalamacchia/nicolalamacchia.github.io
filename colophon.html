Hi

My name is Nicola Lamacchia (/la'makkja/). I was born in 1988 in Apulia, Italy, and still live there. I'm an Electronic Engineering student and a member of the association Puglia Software Open Source, with which I organize the Linux Day in my city every year.

In the spare time I patch buggy GNU/Linux applications, fix broken hardware, design broken hardware, try to receive shortwave radio communications and take photographs, mostly on black and white films, which I develop in my bathroom.
